HOLLER ARCADE

Pull up a stump. Half this thing lives in the hills. The other half lives in the way the hills are stitched together.

Porch Pickin at Sundown

The porch is humming and the mountain keeps time.

Holler Arcade is an art project with one boot on the porch boards and the other on a render pass.

Press it and the holler changes its mind the same way the sky does when the tune turns over.

What it is

Holler Arcade is Appalachian by memory, a little western by horizon line, and playful enough to grin at its own boots.

What it borrows

Hee Haw foolishness, Bill Monroe speed, George Jones ache, Doom shadows, RollerCoaster Tycoon terrain, ASCII scrawl, and sprites with dirt under their nails.

Where the art lives

Nearly half the picture is in the build itself: stacked layers, tree generators, timing loops, hand-shaped ridges, and code doing the same work a brush would do somewhere else.
